Privacy Policy

At FastGoods, safeguarding your privacy is paramount. We collect essential basic details for account creation and personalized services:

These details ensure seamless account management, effective communication, and personalized interactions.

We prioritize your data privacy and security throughout our services.

This App is for 18+

FastGoods is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect or store any information from individuals under 18 years of age. If you are under 18, please do not use this app or provide any personal information.

We Do Not Collect Any Child Information

We are committed to protecting the privacy of children. FastGoods does not collect, use, or store any information from individuals under 18 years of age. If you believe that a child has provided their information to us, please contact us immediately at mdafastgoods@gmail.com.

Location Access

We use only foreground location access and do not use it when the app is closed or in the background. We use location data solely to fetch your source location for making booking easier and faster. Your location information is treated with the utmost care and is not shared with third parties without your explicit consent.
